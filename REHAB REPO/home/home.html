Our services

Myofascial Release

Our myofascial release therapy focuses on alleviating tension and restrictions in the connective tissue (fascia) surrounding muscles. Using gentle, sustained pressure and stretching techniques, our therapists help improve flexibility, reduce pain, and restore optimal function.

Joint Mobilization

Joint mobilization techniques are used to improve the mobility and function of stiff or restricted joints. Through precise movements and manual manipulation, our therapists aim to increase joint range of motion, reduce pain, and enhance overall joint health.

Trigger Point Therapy

Our trigger point therapy targets localized areas of muscle tension and discomfort known as trigger points. Using various manual techniques, including pressure release and stretching, we work to deactivate trigger points, alleviate pain, and restore muscle function.

Silicone Cupping

Silicone cupping therapy involves the application of silicone cups to the skin to create suction and decompress underlying tissues. This technique helps to improve circulation, release muscle tightness, and promote healing. Silicone cupping can be effective in reducing pain, enhancing recovery, and improving overall mobility and function and many more...
